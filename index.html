Growing Up Digital

by Stephen Anaman

This project tells the story of how technology has shaped my life from childhood to now. Through two images and an audio story, I show how my relationship with devices changed from simple games to constant connection through social media.

Childhood – Gaming Days

This image represents my earliest memories with technology: handheld games and simple graphics, before social media and constant notifications.

Teen Years – Always Online

This image shows how my phone and social media became the center of my digital life, with notifications and messages constantly pulling my attention.

Audio Story – Growing Up Digital

This audio piece combines my narration with digital sound effects to tell the story of growing up with technology and how it affects my focus, emotions, and creativity.
